How to use

$ export RAILS_ENV=production
$ bundle
$ rake db:schema:load
$ rake assets:precompile
$ rails s -p 80 &
$ rake scheduler:perform &

Environment

$ export DEFAULT_PROJECT_ACCESS_TOKEN=#your_github_token#
$ export HIPSTER_CI_LOGIN=#your_login#
$ export HIPSTER_CI_PASSWORD=#your_password#
$ export SECRET_KEY_BASE=`rake secret`

Github WebhookProject > Settings > Webhooks & Services > Add webhook

http://#login#:#password#@#your_server_ip#/triggers/github

Example:


http://admin:secret@example.com/triggers/github
